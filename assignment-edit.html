Organic Chemistry II Assignments Edit Assignment

Edit Assignment

Write a formal lab report documenting your synthesis of aspirin (acetylsalicylic acid) through the esterification of salicylic acid with acetic anhydride. Your report must follow the standard scientific format:

  1. Abstract (150 words max)
  2. Introduction with relevant background and hypothesis
  3. Materials and Methods section
  4. Results including yield calculations and melting point data
  5. Discussion analyzing purity and potential sources of error
  6. Conclusion summarizing key findings

Include all calculations for theoretical yield, actual yield, and percent yield. Attach photos of your IR spectra.
