ReDefiners Learning Management System - Acceptable Use Policy
1. Purpose

This Acceptable Use Policy governs the use of the ReDefiners Canvas Learning Management System (LMS) by all authorized users including students, faculty, staff, and administrators. The LMS is provided to support teaching, learning, and administrative activities at the institution.

2. Authorized Use

The LMS may be used for:

  • Accessing and participating in enrolled courses
  • Submitting assignments, quizzes, and other academic work
  • Communicating with instructors and classmates about course-related matters
  • Accessing course materials, grades, and academic resources
  • Participating in discussions, collaborations, and group projects
3. Prohibited Activities

Users shall not:

  • Share login credentials with any other person
  • Submit work that is not their own without proper attribution (plagiarism)
  • Distribute copyrighted materials without authorization
  • Use the platform for commercial purposes unrelated to coursework
  • Engage in harassment, discrimination, or cyberbullying through the platform
  • Attempt to access unauthorized areas of the system or other users' accounts
  • Upload malicious software, viruses, or harmful content
  • Use automated tools to scrape, download, or extract course content in bulk
4. Academic Integrity

All users must uphold academic integrity standards when using the LMS. This includes honest completion of assessments, proper citation of sources, and respect for intellectual property rights. Violations may result in academic disciplinary action as outlined in the Student Code of Conduct.

5. Privacy and Data Protection

The institution respects user privacy and handles personal data in accordance with FERPA regulations and applicable privacy laws. User activity within the LMS may be logged for security and analytics purposes. Instructors may use plagiarism detection services for submitted work.

6. AI-Assisted Learning Tools

The LMS includes AI-powered learning tools. Users should understand that AI responses are generated suggestions and should not be considered definitive academic advice. Use of AI tools must comply with individual course policies set by instructors regarding AI assistance on assignments.

7. Consequences of Violation

Violations of this policy may result in temporary or permanent suspension of LMS access, academic penalties, or referral to the Office of Student Conduct. Severe violations may be subject to legal action.

8. Contact

For questions regarding this policy, contact the Office of Information Technology at it-support@redefiners.edu or the Dean of Students Office at deanofstudents@redefiners.edu.
