Assignment Description

Write a 500-750 word essay in Spanish about your family. This assignment is designed to help you practice using vocabulary related to family members, physical descriptions, and personality traits that we have covered in Chapters 3 and 4.

Your essay should include descriptions of at least four family members using adjectives we have studied. Be sure to use correct gender and number agreement with all adjectives. Include at least three examples of the verb "ser" and three examples of the verb "tener" used in context.

Please double-space your essay and use 12-point Times New Roman font. Include a title at the top of your essay. You may submit your work as a .docx, .pdf, or use the text entry box below. Refer to the rubric for grading criteria.
