Academic Integrity Pledge

ReDefiners World Languages - Honor Code Agreement

Honor Code

As a member of the ReDefiners World Languages learning community, I understand that academic integrity is fundamental to the educational mission of this institution and essential to my personal and intellectual growth.

By signing this pledge, I affirm the following commitments:

1. Original Work: All assignments, essays, projects, and examinations that I submit will represent my own original work and ideas, unless otherwise directed by my instructor for collaborative assignments.

2. Proper Attribution: I will properly cite and attribute all sources, references, and ideas that are not my own, following the citation guidelines provided by my instructor and academic standards.

3. Unauthorized Assistance: I will not give or receive unauthorized assistance on any graded work, including but not limited to examinations, quizzes, homework assignments, and presentations.

4. AI and Translation Tools: I will use artificial intelligence tools and machine translation services only as permitted by my instructor. I understand that using these tools without explicit authorization constitutes a violation of academic integrity.

5. Examination Conduct: During examinations and quizzes, I will not access unauthorized materials, communicate with other students, or use electronic devices unless expressly permitted by my instructor.

6. Reporting Violations: I understand my responsibility to uphold the integrity of the academic community and will report suspected violations of this honor code to my instructor or the appropriate institutional authority.

Consequences of Violations: I understand that violations of academic integrity may result in a range of consequences, including but not limited to: a failing grade on the assignment, a failing grade in the course, a formal report to the Office of Academic Affairs, and potential disciplinary action up to and including suspension or dismissal from the program.

I have read, understood, and agree to abide by all provisions of this Academic Integrity Pledge for the duration of my enrollment in courses offered by ReDefiners World Languages.
