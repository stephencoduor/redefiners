Which verb tense would you use to describe a completed action in the past with a definite beginning and end?

Your answer: Cuando era joven, jugaba al futbol todos los fines de semana.
Question 3 0/3 pts

Complete the sentence: "Ayer, mientras yo _______ (leer), mi hermano _______ (llegar) a casa."

Your answer: leí / llegaba
Correct answer: leía / llegó
Teacher comment: Remember, the ongoing background action uses imperfect (leía), while the interrupting action uses preterite (llegó). Think of it as "while X was happening, Y happened."
