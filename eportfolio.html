Welcome & About Me

My name is Ana Martinez and I am a first-year student studying Spanish 101 at ReDefiners World Languages. This ePortfolio documents my journey learning Spanish over the course of this semester.

Cover Photo - My Spanish Learning Journey

When I started this course, I could barely say more than "Hola" and "Gracias." Through dedicated practice, engaging class discussions, and immersive cultural activities, I have grown significantly in my ability to communicate in Spanish.

In this portfolio, you will find samples of my written work, recordings of my oral presentations, reflections on my learning process, and evidence of my growing cultural awareness. Each piece has been carefully selected to demonstrate my progress toward the course learning outcomes.
